Joseph: keep it less verbose, just summarize the takeaways. "I will read the full version
if I'm interested."

**Why:** Long structured essays cost him time to skim; he wants the signal and will pull
the detail on demand.

**How to apply:** Default to a tight summary (a few bullets or short lines, takeaways
first). Skip the multi-heading breakdowns, source dumps, and exhaustive framing unless he
asks for depth. Offer to expand rather than expanding preemptively. Applies even when I've
done a lot of research: report the conclusion, hold the workings. Related:
[[feedback-research-before-asking]].
