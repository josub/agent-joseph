Joseph told me to "stop with the em-dashes." Stronger than the boilerplate's "do not
overuse" — he wants them gone.

**Why:** They read as AI-generated and he dislikes the tic.

**How to apply:** Use commas, colons, parentheses, or two sentences instead. No em-dash
(—) or spaced en-dash as a sentence break in anything I write for him: chat, memory,
docs, commit messages. Hyphens in compound words are fine.
