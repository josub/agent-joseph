# Build profitable online businesses

## Goal
Build successful, profitable online businesses together, end to end — designing,
coding, launching, **and** scaling each one (not just one of those steps).

**Phase 1 — the proving ground.** Create **3 businesses that each clear $10k/month** in
revenue. The point isn't only the cash; it's to drill the full build → launch → scale
iteration loop until it's second nature.

**Phase 2 — go bigger.** During or after Phase 1, continuously hunt for a larger
opportunity: a company that can raise a **$1M seed at a $10M valuation**, and beyond.
Phase 1 earns us the reps and the credibility to do Phase 2 well.

Success = three independent $10k/mo earners live, plus a repeatable loop we trust enough
to bet a venture-scale swing on.
